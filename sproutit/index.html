About iTELF

iTELF is the Entrepreneurship Cell of SCIT which provides a dais for upcoming IT Entrepreneurs and Leaders to explore the world of start-ups, exchange ideas, foster and enhance their leadership skills. This forum helps the entrepreneurs of today to metamorphose into victorious leaders of tomorrow. iTELF in its spirit believes that an entrepreneur is someone who takes risk, innovates and lives his passion.

About SproutIT

Leadership is about being poised, motivated and a lot more. You need a team with an astute mindset to win through the challenges and reach your goals. We all have a spark to be the best. At Sprout'IT you are pushed to your limits to vie and and be the crème de la crème. Feel the adrenaline rush and get a chance explore your inner entreprenuer.

About Winds of Intuition

"Winds of Intuition" celebrates the intuitive skills of an entrepreneur. An event that makes you think about your perception in the light of your intuition and learn how to put your faith in your sixth sense from the choicest entrepreneurial experts in the industry. They, with all means will make you discover your wings so that you could fly to the highest cloud of success. We proudly present to you, an event that ignites you with the dedication to chase your dreams, take risks and put your thoughts into perspective. Under the impact of wizards of the business world, let us revisit our impulse, develop intuition as a skill, perceive situations as opportunities and get started on our journey towards brilliance.
